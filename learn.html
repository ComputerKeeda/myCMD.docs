Markdown (.mk) Tutorials

Headings

Headings in .mk are like h1,h2 tags of html, here # means h1, ## means h2, ### means h3 and so on e.g.


# Its h1 tag

Its h1 tag


## Its h2 tag

Its h2 tag


# Its h3 tag

Its h3 tag


#### Its h4 tag

Its h4 tag


##### Its h5 tag
Its h5 tag

###### Its h4 tag
Its h6 tag

Bold

To make Text Bold put the text between **. e.g.


**i would something just like this**
i would something just like this

Italic

To make content italis, put tex between _. e.g.-

_its italic text_
its italic text

Line

to make like like we make with hr tag, we use ---

---

Link

to give links in .md files we use [content](url) instade of a href = ''

[google](https://www.google.com)
google

Image

To insert image in .md files we use ![alter text](img link) insted of img src
